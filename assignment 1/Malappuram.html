MALAPPURAM


Malappuram is situated in the mid land area of the state. As the name suggests, it is covered with small mountains of lush greenery, bonded with several freshwater streams flowing through the city. Kadalundi Puzha, a major river in Kerala is flowing around the city.The best season to visit Malappuram is during the months of September to March as the weather conditions are quite pleasant.
